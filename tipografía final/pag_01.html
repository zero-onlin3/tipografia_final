the fiends

Meet the little creatures in charge of the show, the fiends. With their curious cat-like appearance, distinct in character and design, at least one of these little guys are sure to steal your heart!


Rocky

Based on the classics tale of Frankenstein, Rocky the frankencat is a shy mute fiend who spends most their time on his own. With a pink fur and bright green stitches, they might seem very out of place, but he has a special talent to blend into his surroundings.

Rocky was born after five ardous hours of work at the Creator's lab. Coat made from the softest fur available, Rocky is mute and partially blind, communicating through sign language and other general gestures. This freaky fiend is sure to keep you the best company at the most silent times.

Tres

Based on the mythos of the vampire, Tres the vampcat is a funky little guy who loves rock music and sleep. With a white fur, a big red heart and staple accessories, this chatterbox makes lovely company to the social kinds.

Tres was resurected in a full moon by the Creator, through an ancient ritual, this kitty was bitten and buried, under the basis that he was going to become danger to town. The Creator gave Tres a second and even third chance, hence the name.
