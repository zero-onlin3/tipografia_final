It all started with a college project, in which three charming characters where designed in the full spirit of spooky season. It soon became a small passion project, producing plushies and furthermore a videogame. As time goes on, Freaky Fiends has the mission of expanding to become a staple character brand.

Please welcome, Tres, Eden and Rocky into your life. They might be small but they are mighty, with distinct looks and characteristics, it might prove hard to pick a favorite!

Dive into their world ➜

Follow Tres in an interesting adventure across the woods in order to get to the rest of the fiends while collecting precious stones! It seems easy but watch out for the bats and pumpkins, they might not be nice!
